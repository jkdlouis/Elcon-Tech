About US

Elcon Electronics is a professional manufacturer of aluminum electric capacitors. The head office locates in Shaokwan. Total staff of the group is 400people, the factory covers an area of 40000 square meters. Since foundation in 1990, Elcon Electronics has constantly been involved in development, production, and sales of aluminum capacitors that are essential for electronic equipment. Elcon Electronics possess more than 25 years experience of designing, manufacturing and sales of aluminum electric capacitors. Also we established a set of strict quality inspection process to ensure that the products can achieve the perfect quality. Manufacturing technology is mature and stable. Product specifications complete, Full range of solid and liquid with long life, high capacity, low impedance, Ultra low impedance, abbreviated form of special products and have high temperature, high voltage characteristics in working. Our products are widely used in Power adapters, chargers, communications, consumer electronics, computer, LED lighting, security, smart electricity, medical equipment and automotive electronics and other fields. Elcon Electronics has always been adhering to the core business philosophy of ‘Dare first, integrity and win-win’, through the efforts of all staff, we strengthen our core competitive advantages in the industry chain, providing the customer with perfect products and the first-class service continuously, Also we are committed to create a platform for the employee to realize their beautiful dreams. Elcon will build a model of qualified enterprise.

History

  • 1990 Established Aijiang Industrial Co., Ltd .
  • 1990 Founded Lingda Electronics Co., Ltd. in Yangzhou.
  • 1994 Founded Aigao Electronics Co. Ltd in Nanjing.
  • 1994 Established the Meijiang Electronics Co. Ltd. in Dongguan Huang Jiang.(Passed certification of THOMSO from France）
  • 1995 Cooperated with SHOEI and supply OEM service for SHOEI, mainly for Japanese customers.
  • 1996 Cooperated with several Japan cooperation with OEM service, including SOSHIN, TOSHIN, SHOEI, NIC and so on.
  • 1998 The establishment of the Shenzhen Aijiang Electronics Co. Ltd.
  • 2000 Established Dongguan Ai Jiang Electronics Factory.
  • 2002 Established Dongguan Li Jiang factory.
  • 2004 Begin the Research and production of Polymer aluminum electric capacitors.
  • 2014 Completed the resources integration and founded Shaoguan Fushida Electronic Technology Co. Ltd. in Guangdon Province as our new production base.
